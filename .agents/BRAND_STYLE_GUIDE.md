# Brand & Style Guide - Sans Finance

## 1. Brand Identity
**App Name:** Sans Finance
**Core Value:** A modern, streamlined activity and state tracking application.
**Aesthetic:** Clean, professional, and "alive" with subtle animations and mesh gradients.

## 5. UI Components & Patterns

### 5.1 Sans Finance Cards
- **Shape:** Large (24dp rounded).
- **Border:** 1dp linear gradient from Primary (60% alpha) to Tertiary (40% alpha).
- **Padding:** 24dp internal padding.

### 5.2 Primary Buttons
- **Height:** 58dp.
- **Font Weight:** Black (900).
- **Shape:** Medium (18dp rounded).
- **Interaction:** Includes haptic feedback (LongPress) on click.

### 5.3 Status Badges
- **Active State:** Tertiary color pulse animation with radial rings.
- **Inactive State:** Slate (Secondary) color, static.
- **Shape:** Pill-shaped (CircleShape).
- **Text:** Uppercase with 1.2sp letter spacing.

### 5.4 Mesh Gradients
- Used in headers to create a dynamic feel.
- Combines animated Indigo and Emerald radial gradients with low alpha (8-12%).

### 5.5 Section Headers
- **Text:** Uppercase.
- **Color:** Slate 500.
- **Spacing:** 1.5sp letter spacing.
